About Us

At Peak Plus, we design games that combine casual fun with meaningful brain engagement. Triple Joy 3D was crafted for players who enjoy matching puzzles with a twist. Instead of flat grids, players interact with vibrant, rotating 3D piles of objects—making every level a tactile, visual, and strategic experience.
The game is optimized for mobile play with quick sessions that offer both relaxation and challenge. Whether you're looking to unwind or sharpen your mind, Triple Joy 3D delivers.
